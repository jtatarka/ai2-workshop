User Interface Design Fundamentals

A great app must be well designed.

Lots of terms...

  • Design (simply put)
  • User Experience Design
  • Interaction Design
  • User Interface Design

"Most people make the mistake of thinking design is what it looks like... That's not what design is. It's not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works."

- Steve Jobs

Design is how it works.

Steps to Design a Great App

  • üì± Create wireframes to visualize ideas
  • üì± Mock up important features
  • üì± Perform usability testing
  • üì± Iterate the design process!

Wireframing

  • Create simple drawings of your interface
  • Don't include much detail or ornamentation
  • Keep it simple and uncomplicated
Image source and more info at cacoo.com

Mockups

  • Create a more detailed visual design
  • The way you want your final app to look
  • Be sure to view them on a device!
Image source and more examples at behance.net

Usability Testing

  • Type of research used to improve app design
  • Doesn't even have to be anything formal
    • üëâ Ask questions about wireframes, watch user
  • Sometimes, though, it is more structured
    • üëâ Conduct surveys, A/B testing, focus groups

Iteration!

  • No on gets it right on the first try üòÅ
  • Make changes based on testing feedback
  • Leverage user research and your observations
  • Collaborate with other designers and developers
